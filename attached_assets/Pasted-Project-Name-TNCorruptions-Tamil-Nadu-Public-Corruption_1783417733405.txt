Project Name

TNCorruptions - Tamil Nadu Public Corruption Complaint & Transparency Portal

Objective

Develop a modern, enterprise-grade, secure web application called TNCorruptions that enables citizens to submit corruption complaints against government departments in Tamil Nadu.

The system should route complaints automatically to the respective department based on the selected department, district, taluk, village, and office.

The platform should support verification workflows, evidence management, analytics, RTI tracking, whistleblower protection, complaint status tracking, and role-based administration.

The UI should be similar to modern government portals with excellent UX, mobile responsiveness, dark/light mode, multilingual support (Tamil & English), and enterprise security.
